Tourist Place in Purwokerto



1.

Baturraden

Baturraden has a very amazing natural scenery, namely the expanse of Mount Slamet on the north side. In addition, there are many choices of tourist rides, which is a distinct advantage as the attraction of this tourist spot. You can find rides such as swimming pools, water bikes, fish therapy and hot springs there The mini zoo available there can also be an alternative for you. In addition, the experience of enjoying the extraordinary scenery of the beautiful and cool mountains is something that will definitely be very difficult to forget from this trip.

2.

Bukit Agaran

A very interesting and very beautiful photo spot can be an option for those of you who like to hunt for charming cloud views as the background for your photos. The expansive blue sky combined with the green jungle below looks amazing like a nature painting.

3.

Curug Jenggala

The place is quite hidden. But the scenery that is presented is very beautiful. There is a waterfall that flows fast there and spills onto the rocks below so that it forms a river with shades of milky white water due to the small volume of water but has a strong current. There is a bridge that connects the right and left sides of the river so that it adds to the exotic scenery there. Besides you can take pictures on this bridge, there is one more photo spot that is no less cool. A heart-shaped piece of wood was intentionally provided right on the plain opposite the waterfall.

4.

Bukit Bintang

Still in the Baturraden area, precisely on Jl. Prince Limboro, there is a hill called Bukit Bintang. If you come at night, the beautiful night view of Purwokerto can be seen clearly. We can enjoy the view there while relaxing and prepare thick clothes because the night is cold.
